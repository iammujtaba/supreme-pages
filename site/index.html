Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Quick answers on nationwide supply, dispatch, MOQ, GST billing and retail support.

Ask wholesale desk ↗
Do you supply bicycle parts across India?

Yes. We supply retailers, wholesalers and distributors across India from our Khalilabad and Ludhiana dispatch warehouses.

How soon is a confirmed wholesale order dispatched?

Confirmed wholesale orders are dispatched the next day. Transit and delivery time depend on the destination and transport service.

What is the minimum wholesale order quantity?

MOQ depends on the product, brand and packing. Send your parts list and approximate quantity for the applicable packing and quotation.

Do you provide GST invoices for wholesale orders?

Yes. GST billing is available. Share your firm and GST details when confirming the order.

Where are your office and warehouses located?

Our office and one warehouse are in Khalilabad, Uttar Pradesh. A second dispatch warehouse operates in Ludhiana, Punjab; its address is not published for customer visits.

Do you also serve retail customers?

Yes. Retail bicycle sales, everyday parts and service support remain available at our Khalilabad store. +91 78888 98988. This number is dedicated to wholesale enquiries across India.
