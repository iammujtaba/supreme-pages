Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Everything else is one WhatsApp message away — stock, sizes, prices and delivery.

Ask on WhatsApp ↗
Do you deliver in Khalilabad and nearby areas?

Yes. Same-day local delivery is often possible in and around Khalilabad when stock and distance allow. For wholesale bicycle parts we arrange delivery across India — confirm timing on WhatsApp before you order.

Which cycle brands and models do you keep?

Stock changes through the season. Often-requested models include Hercules Storm, SkyIndia Bullet tricycles and Panda kids’ cycles, alongside everyday city and electric cycles. Message us on WhatsApp for today’s models and prices.

Do you sell bicycle parts in retail as well as wholesale?

Yes. Riders can buy tyres, tubes, locks, pumps and everyday spares at the counter, and retailers or wholesalers can order parts from brands like Bhogal, Masco, Trax, Hatora, Bedrock and Ralco in bulk with GST billing.

Can I get my cycle assembled or repaired here?

Yes. Every new cycle is assembled and adjusted before handover, and we support regular service and repair work — bring your cycle to the store or call ahead.

What are the store timings?

We are open 9:00 AM to 7:30 PM every day except Friday, when the store stays closed. On public holidays a quick call before visiting is recommended.

How do I place a wholesale order?

Send your parts list with brands and quantities to our wholesale WhatsApp on +91 78888 98988. The team confirms availability, price and dispatch across India. GST invoice available.
